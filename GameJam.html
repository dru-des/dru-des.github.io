Dalhousie GameJam 2018

Winners of the Best In-game Easter Eggs

48 HOURS. 5 STUDENTS. 1 GAME.

The Game Is On.

When I was a first year, in November 2018, I took part in GameJam, a 48 hour game development competition at Dalhousie. Our team had to learn making 3D models, transferring our coding skills into Unity, and finally porting the app to Android and iOS. Without the help and guidance from Ubisoft Halifax's Aaron Stafford, such a monumental task would not have been possible back then. We worked under pressure to learn new technologies, but we delivered. Best in-game Easter Eggs! And the best part is that I was involved directly making the 3D models, music samples and more.
