THE HAPPY COMMUNITY PROJECT REDESIGN

Happy Community Project is a Nova Scotia based not-for-profit organization that aims to bring communities together, working off donations from people like you or me.

Behind Episode 1

As a part of the course CSCI 3691, our team was paired with a client, Mr. Barry Braun. He was the founder of the Happy Community Project, and approached us about a redesign of his website. Over twelve weeks, our team worked to create some of the best and most user friendly elements for the website. The final results are below, but please feel encouraged to check out the full final website at www.happycommunityproject.com.
